2012 Delhi Electricity Consumption Analysis

Hover to View Detailed Electricity Consumption ( Max/Min are identified in bold borders)

We are investigating disparities in electricity access and consumption within Delhi. We have electricity load data at the meter/feeder level (measured every 15 minutes, daily from 2010 - 2013*) from the three private electricity distribution companies (NDPL North Delhi Power Limited, BYPL BSES Yamuna Power Limited, BRPL BSES Rajdhani Power Limited) and the New Delhi Municipal Committee (NDMC) and Military Engineer Services (MES).

  • From 2010-03-29 to 2013-03-10, also partial data from 2008 and 2009

The data that we have also calculates weekly net consumption by grid and these grids have been assigned names of actual locations in Delhi which we have mapped below (grid "Pappankalan-1" and "Pappankalan-2" were merged into 1 location, and NDMC and MES were added to the list, to make a total of 22 grid locations). Based on these locations, we created Voronoi polygons to approximate the areas being serviced by these grids, and these "grid areas" (dashed lines) have been overlaid on a map of Delhi and its 9 districts.

We are currently looking for population, income, and other socio-economic/demographic data at the district/sub-district level that can then be associated with the weekly electricity consumption data recorded for each grid.
