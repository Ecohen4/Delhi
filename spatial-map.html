Spatial Map of Weekly Electricity Consumption in Delhi by Grid for 2012

Grid names were geo-coded to retrieve lat-long coordinates. Voronoi polygons were computed on the set of lat-long coordinates to approximate the area served by each grid. Move the slider to view changes over one year, at weekly resolution. '1' on the slider corresponds to the week ending 2012-01-08 and '52' corresponds to the week ending 2012-12-30. Values are in KWh per person per week.
