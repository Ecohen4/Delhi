Calendar Heatmap of Daily Electricity Consumption in Delhi for 2012

Each square is a day. Darker color represents higher energy consumption. Units are GWh. Hover to view details. Annual maxima and minima are identified by bold borders.

Data Visualization: Christopher Buckingham, Wenjuan Deng and Sou Min Sonia Lee.
Project Lead: Elliot Cohen, Ph.D.
Data Source: State Load Dispatch Center of Delhi
